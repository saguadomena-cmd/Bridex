Legal structuring — SPV setup
A Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) is created for each property. The SPV legally owns the asset and links it to BRIK tokens, giving investors real, enforceable rights over cash flows and capital gains.

Asset types

Any property.
Any country.

BRIDEX tokenizes all types of real estate — residential, commercial, and development projects — from any jurisdiction in the world.

Residential properties
Apartments, houses, luxury villas. Tokenize rental income rights and give global investors access to residential real estate in any city.
Example: Owner tokenizes a 100,000 EUR apartment in Barcelona — 1,000 BRIK at 100€ each. Min. buy: 1 BRIK. Investors receive monthly rent in BRIK automatically.
Commercial real estate
Office buildings, retail spaces, warehouses and shopping centers. Higher yields, longer leases, institutional-grade assets accessible from 100 EUR.
Example: A financial institution tokenizes loans secured by commercial properties — each token tracks capital and interest rights automatically.
Development projects
Real estate developers attract capital efficiently during early phases. Blockchain provides clear milestone transparency — investors track progress on-chain.
Example: Developer tokenizes a new residential complex — investors receive tokens that convert to property fractions on completion.
Rental income streams
Tokenize only the income rights of a property — without transferring ownership. Perfect for owners who want to access capital while keeping the asset.
Example: Owner of a rented office building tokenizes 5 years of rental income — investors receive monthly BRIK payments, owner keeps the property.
Real estate debt tokens
Loans secured by properties are grouped into a digital format — each token tracks capital and interest rights. Smart contracts manage payment schedules automatically.
Example: A bank tokenizes mortgage-backed loans — investors access fixed-income products with real-time auditability and automatic interest payments.
Hotel & hospitality
Hotels, serviced apartments and holiday rentals. Tokenize room revenue and occupancy income — share the upside of the global tourism economy with any investor.
Example: 5-star hotel in central London tokenizes 20% of its room revenue — investors receive weekly BRIK distributions proportional to occupancy.
